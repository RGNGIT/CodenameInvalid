WispGUI 1.1
===========

- Smart component creator : just press Control + Space.
- 5 New GUI Shaders to achieve a Glass, Frosted Glass and Glassmorphism Effects.
- 2 New GUI Shaders to generate linear and radial gradients.
- Live style update in playmode for the component selected in hierarchy.
- 1 New style : Minimaliste.
- New style parameters : Horizontal and Vertical padding.
